Abstract#==#We present a new treebank of English and French technical forum content which
has been annotated for grammatical errors and phrase structure. This double
annotation allows us to empirically measure the effect of errors on parsing
performance. While it is slightly easier to parse the corrected versions of the
forum sentences, the errors are not the main factor in making this kind of text
hard to parse.
