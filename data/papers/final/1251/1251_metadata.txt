Abstract#==#We revisit the classical logical notion of generation/parsing reversibility in
terms of feasible probabilistic sampling, and argue for an implementation based
on finite-state factors. We propose a modular decomposition that reconciles
generation accuracy with parsing robustness and allows the introduction of
dynamic contextual factors.
