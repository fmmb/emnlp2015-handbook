Abstract#==#This paper introduces a convolutional sentence kernel based on word embeddings.
Our kernel overcomes the sparsity issue that arises when classifying short
documents or in case of little training data. Experiments on six sentence
datasets showed statistically significant higher accuracy over the standard
linear kernel with n-gram features and other proposed models.
