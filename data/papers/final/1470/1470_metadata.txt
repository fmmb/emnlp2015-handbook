Abstract#==#We introduce "Trans-gram", a simple
and computationally-efficient method to simultaneously learn and align
word-embeddings for a variety of languages, using only monolingual data and a
smaller set of sentence-aligned data.
We use our new method to compute aligned word-embeddings for twenty-one
languages using English as a pivot language.
We show that some linguistic features are aligned across languages for which we
do not have aligned data, even though those properties do not exist in the
pivot language.
We also achieve state of the art results on standard cross-lingual text
classification and word translation tasks.
