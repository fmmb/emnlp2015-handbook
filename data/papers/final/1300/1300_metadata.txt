Abstract#==#We investigate the role that geometric, textual and visual features play in the
task of predicting a preposition that links two visual entities depicted in an
image. The task is an important part of the subsequent process of generating
image descriptions. We explore the prediction of prepositions for a pair of
entities, both in the case when the labels of such entities are known and
unknown. In all situations we found clear evidence that all three features
contribute to the prediction task.
