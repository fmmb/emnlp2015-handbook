Abstract#==#It is in PropBank's ARGM annotation of clausal adjuncts that
sentential semantics meets discourse relation annotation in the Penn
Discourse TreeBank. This paper discusses complementarities between the
two annotation systems: How PropBank ARGM annotation can be used to
seed annotation of additional discourse relations in the PDTB, and how PDTB
annotation can be used to refine or enrich PropBank ARGM
annotation.
