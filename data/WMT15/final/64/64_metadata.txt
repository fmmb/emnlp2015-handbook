Abstract#==#This paper presents the machine translation systems submitted by the Abu-MaTran
project for the Finnish–English language pair at the WMT 2015 translation
task. We tackle the lack of resources and complex morphology of the Finnish
language by
(i) crawling parallel and monolingual data from the Web and (ii) applying
rule-based and unsupervised methods for morphological segmentation. Several
statistical machine translation approaches are evaluated and then combined to
obtain our final submissions, which are the top performing English-to-Finnish
unconstrained (all automatic metrics) and constrained (BLEU), and
Finnish-to-English constrained (TER) systems.
