Abstract#==#This paper describes the syntax-based systems built at the University of
Edinburgh for the WMT 2015 shared translation task. We developed systems for
all language pairs except French-English. This year we focused on: translation
out of English using tree-to-string models; continuing to improve our
English-German system; and source-side morphological segmentation of Finnish
using Morfessor.
