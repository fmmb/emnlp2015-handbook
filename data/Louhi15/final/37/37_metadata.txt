Abstract#==#The paper illustrates the results of a case study aimed at investigating and
enhancing the accessibility of Italian health–related documents by relying on
advanced NLP techniques, with particular attention to informed consent forms.
Results achieved show that the features automatically extracted from the
linguistically annotated text and ranging across different levels of linguistic
description have a high discriminative power in order to guarantee a reliable
readability assessment.
