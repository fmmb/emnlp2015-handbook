Abstract#==#Multimodal integration of visual and linguistic data is a longstanding but
crucial challenge for modeling human understanding. We propose a framework that
uses an unsupervised bitext alignment method to integrate visual and linguistic
data. We present an empirical study of the various parameters of the framework.
Our results exceed baselines using both exact and delayed temporal
correspondence. The resulting alignments can be used for image classification
and retrieval.
